COVID-19: Self Isolation Help

What is this tool for?

If you or someone you know is self isolating due to the outbreak of Coronavirus and need access to food, medicine or support, you can use this tool to request help from a local volunteer group in the United Kingdom.

If you have breathing difficulties or severe symptoms do not use this tool. Instead, dial 999 immediately to seek emergency help.

To request assistance for you, or someone else please click 'Continue' below
