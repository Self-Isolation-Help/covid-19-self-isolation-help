To register as a Volunteer and connect with people in need in your area, please follow the instructions below. All volunteer registration requests are vetted manually.

How to register

Copy and paste the text below with your answers to the email address: volunteers@selfisolationhelp.co.uk

================================

First name

Do you have a criminal record?

Your DBS number (if applicable)

Email address
